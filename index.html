About the Project

The Lost Potential Tracker was created by the ONE Campaign and partners to illustrate the urgency of the global learning crisis and what's at stake. The figures are a result of combining World Bank and UNESCO data on learning poverty with UN population estimates. To develop accurate estimates for the methodology, ONE set up an Advisory Council of stakeholders in the education sector.

But, funding alone is not enough!

It must be invested well. Here are some recommendations for how the funding should be spent.

Every child should have access to an education and stay in school

Children who are out of school are much less likely to learn.
Learn more

Every child should have an equal start

Early childhood education helps prepare a child for the rest of their education journey. But many children are unable to access it.
Learn more

Every child should have a supportive learning environment tailored to their needs while in school

Children without the right resources in place to meet their needs are less likely to learn.
Learn more

Every child should have good teaching

Teachers play one of the biggest roles in a child's education journey. They need to be equipped with the right resources to provide good teaching.
Learn more

So what can be done?

  • Adequately fund education from all sources
  • Ensure that funding is spent effectively with a focus on those most at risk
